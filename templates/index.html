To calculate the Doomsday of a given year:

  • How many times can 12 fit in the last two digits of the year?
  • Find the difference between the (last two digits of the year) and the product of (calculation 1 and 12)
  • How many times can 4 fit in calculation 2?
  • What is the current century's Doomsday?
  • Add up all the results
  • Take the final calculation modulo 7
